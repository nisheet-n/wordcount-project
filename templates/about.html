ABOUT


This is a Word count app which counts the number of words in a document or passage of text. It is a useful tool for writers to see the count of the total number of words in their article.
The user will enter the text of their choice in the text box provided in the homepage. Then the text will get parsed and then the total number of words along with the number of characters, sentences, paragraphs and top keywords will be displayed to the user.
